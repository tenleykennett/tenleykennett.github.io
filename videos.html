Art & Edits

When I was 13, I started a fanpage on Instagram for one of my favorite TV shows— Stranger Things— making edits with phone apps like CapCut and Videostar. Eventually, I transitioned to Adobe After Effects, teaching myself everything from visual effects to motion graphics and transitions. This adventure helped me grow my creativity, sharpen my digital design skills, and even build a following. Before I knew it, I was managing a tutorial account with over 60,000 followers, all while I was barely a teenager. Here are some of my favorite edits!
