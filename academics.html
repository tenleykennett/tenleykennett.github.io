Virginia Flora & Fauna Database

Fall 2025

Academic Project

My team & I designed and implemented a relational database to store and manage information on Virginia’s native flora and fauna. Our goal was to amass a centralized dataset of Virginia’s native species in one location. The project focused on database schema design and efficient querying using SQL. I worked with PostgreSQL and pgAdmin to create tables, relationships, and queries that support filtering by different parameters such as species, habitat, and native status.
