Pollinators In Your Yard Event Map

ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator

2026

This map was made in collaboration with Harrisonburg's Public Works department. I was tasked with creating a map for an annual event: Pollinators in Your Yard. The map shows the locations of the education stations and event talks in downtown Harrisonburg. It shows education stations and event talks scattered across downtown Harrisonburg, covering three key locations: the Ice House Building, Pale Fire Brewing Company, and Drifters Café. It was my first time ever doing indoor mapping, and it was tough to balance mapping both the insides and outsides of the buildings. In the end, I'm really happy with how it turned out.
