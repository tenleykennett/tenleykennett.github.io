Massanutten Terrain Map

ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

2026

Kennelly et al. (2020), Zenodo.

U.S. Geological Survey, National Hydrography Dataset (NHD), HU-8 02070001 (2023)

I made this map for one of my cartography courses. The goal was to get experience with terrain mapping, hypsometric tints, and working with DEMs. I used Illustrator and Photoshop to fine-tune the labels and subtle halos around them.
