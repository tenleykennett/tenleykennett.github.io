Parques Nacionales. España

The idea for this map came to me in a dream. I love cross stitching and I love maps, so I think my subconscious mind put these two together. Under the guidance of the incredible John Nelson, I was able to create an awesome representation of Spain's national parks. I had a great time experimenting with new techniques in ArcGIS, and I learned a lot from just this one map.
